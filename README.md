WaitWise – Smart Hospital Queue Management System
1. Problem Statement
In hospitals and clinics, managing patient queues manually often creates confusion, long waiting times, and poor visibility for patients. Patients frequently do not know when their turn will come, which leads to repeated inquiries at reception. Receptionists also struggle to prioritize emergency patients and manage live queue updates efficiently. Traditional token systems lack real-time visibility, dynamic prioritization, and estimated waiting time tracking. This creates inefficiency in hospital workflows and negatively impacts patient experience.
2. Solution
To solve this problem, I built WaitWise, a smart hospital queue management system. WaitWise provides a live queue dashboard for receptionists and a real-time patient panel for patients. The receptionist can add patients, assign emergency priority, call the next patient, cancel patients, and update average consultation time. Patients can view the current patient being served, the next patient in line, queue ahead count, and estimated waiting time. This improves transparency, reduces confusion, and optimizes hospital queue flow.
